# Privacy Policy — Sort YouTube Playlist by Duration

_Last updated: 2026-07-18_

**This extension collects no personal data and transmits no data to the developer or any third
party.**

## What the extension does with data

- **Your settings** (sort order, pacing, filters, and similar preferences) are stored only in
  your own browser's local storage on youtube.com. They never leave your device and are never
  sent anywhere.
- **Your playlist data** (video titles, durations, and playlist item identifiers) is read from
  the YouTube page you are already viewing, solely to compute the new order, and reorder requests
  are sent only to YouTube's own servers using your existing YouTube session. This is the same
  data YouTube already shows you on the page.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does not send any data to the developer (LunarWerx) or to any third party.
- It does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or fingerprinting of any kind.
- It does not collect personally identifiable information, financial information, authentication
  credentials, health information, personal communications, location, or browsing history.
- It does not sell or transfer user data to anyone.
- It does not use or transfer user data for creditworthiness or lending purposes.
- It does not download or execute any remote code; all of its code ships inside the extension
  package.

## Network activity

The extension makes network requests only to `www.youtube.com` — the same site you are on — to
read your playlist and to reorder it on your behalf, authenticated by your existing YouTube login.
No other servers are ever contacted.
